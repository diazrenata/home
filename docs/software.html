Software and data products

R packages

birdsize

Functions to simulate size measurements for real or imagined bird populations.

portalr

Tools to download & access summaries of data from the Portal Project.

LDATS

Combines Latent Dirichlet Allocation with Bayesian changepoint-analysis of time-series to detect rapid transitions in ecological community composition. Building on the analysis in Christensen et al. 2018.

MATSS

Macroecological Analysis of Time-series Structure. Load numerous ecological timeseries datasets in a standardized format, and build flexible piplines using drake or similar packages.

feasiblesads

Draw samples from the feasible set for a species abundance distribution.

cvlt

(In dev.) Cross-validation functions to apply LDATS to short, high-dimensional timeseries.

Data products

PortalData

Partially automated system for quality assurance and open-access to regularly-updated data on desert rodents, plants, and weather associated with the Portal Project. See the paper describing the workflow and accompanying data paper hosted on bioRxiv.

Replication reports

replicate-becs

Replication of Ernest (2005): Body size, energy use, and community structure of small mammals. In prep, for submission to ReScience C.
